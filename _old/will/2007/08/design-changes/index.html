Design Changes

by @im_a_muppet on August 5, 2007

Over the past few meetings our design has changed quite a bit. For starters we decided not to use the diamond formation to control frequency. Instead we will just manually move the handles ourselves. We no longer have additional amplitude or frequency dials.

The current version of the project just has sliders on magnets. These sliders can be moved up and down. Then the magnets can be moved from left to right.
